Fruit Catchers

Baby Monkey & Hungry Gorilla are fighting for their survivals in the jungle. Grab your fruits quickly and avoid looming predators, whoever gathers more fruits will survive the coming hurricane!

  • This is a 2-player game
  • Each game lasts 30 seconds
  • Whoever has the most points when time is up wins
  • You can move Baby Monkey with Arrow keys & Hungry Gorilla with WASD keys
  • Each fruit has certain points: Banana = 5 pts, Apple = 10 pts, Grape = 15 pts & Pineapple = 20 pts
  • Avoid the apple worm and the eagles: Apple Worm = -10 pts & Eagle = -15 pts if they catch you
